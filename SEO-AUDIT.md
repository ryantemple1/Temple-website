# Temple Landscaping SEO Audit
## Focus: Ranking in SW Calgary for landscaping and related services

---

## Current Score: 5.5/10

### What's working
- Good page titles and meta descriptions on every page
- Sitemap, robots.txt, and canonical URLs all set up
- LocalBusiness structured data exists
- Good image optimization with Next.js
- Trust signals (500+ homes, 8+ years, $3M insured, 108 reviews)
- 56 SW Calgary neighbourhoods listed (but hidden)

### What's not working
- Only targeting generic "Calgary" keywords, not SW Calgary specifically
- Neighbourhood list is hidden (display:none), search engines may ignore it
- No neighbourhood-specific landing pages
- No blog or seasonal content
- Incomplete business schema (missing street address)
- No review schema markup
- Service page titles don't emphasize location

---

## Bottom Line

The site looks great and the technical foundation is solid. The biggest missed opportunity is **location-specific content**. You have 56 SW Calgary neighbourhoods listed but they're hidden and unused. Creating even 10 neighbourhood landing pages could increase organic traffic by 30-50%.

The second biggest gap is **keyword specificity**. Everything targets generic "Calgary" when "SW Calgary" + specific neighbourhood names would capture much more targeted, higher-converting traffic.
